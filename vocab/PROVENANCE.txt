vocab/types.json is a verbatim snapshot of the shared connection-type
vocabulary from the orchestrator repo:

  repo: Data-Flow-Advisory/orchestrator
  ref:  feat/drift-gate
  path: types.json

It is vendored (rather than fetched at CI time) so the port conformance
check is hermetic and does not depend on a moving feature branch. Refresh
it when the upstream vocabulary changes. Do not hand-edit — locally
proposed additions live in vocab/proposed_types.json and must be
upstreamed into the orchestrator's types.json before they are official.

The connection standard itself: orchestrator@feat/drift-gate/CONNECTORS.md
